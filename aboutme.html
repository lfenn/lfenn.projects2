A Little About Me

My name is K. Finn, I am currently a college student, and nonetheless a millennial. I grew up in a family that never talked much about politics, so I was never informed about what was going on around me. Furthermore, all my political opinions came from my teachers, or other mentors. Who were severely biased as well. In turn, when I turned 18 I made it my goal to make sure that I was informed whenever it came to my country. This includes, the upcoming election as well. I was inspired to make this website, because of a project that I had in one of my classes. Nonetheless, I think that it will be fun to document how my voting process is as things move along.
