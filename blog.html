My Experience so far

Day:10/25/2016

I voted back in the primaries, a couple months back. I was mistakenly taken to a republican primary, although the candidate that I wanted to vote for was a democrat. Nonetheless, I was left with the decision to vote for trump, cruz, and johnson. At that time, I was terribly misinformed and ended up voting for Johnson. However, after reviewing his policies, I don’t exactly agree with what he believes. Although, I must say that I don’t regret my decision. I have been silently, reading up on each candidate and their policies, and what I best feel correlates with my beliefs. (yes, I am voting on my conscious.) Furthermore, I also want to take in consideration who I best feel is a leader.

Good News
